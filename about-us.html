What We Do

We help good people do hard things. That usually means:

  • Running campaigns
  • Clarifying strategy
  • Making research usable
  • Supporting public engagement
  • Writing the words that make it all land

We work small, move fast, and show up when it counts.

Strategic Messaging

We craft clear, powerful messages and shape them into stories that stick, turning complex ideas into compelling narratives while making sure that the right message reaches the right audience at the right time. To be clear we are trying to make change, not just give you advice.

We develop the messaging frameworks that maintain credibility under pressure and advance priorities through shifting landscapes.

Examples:
- Reframed an environmental issue as a public health emergency via media & grassroots storytelling
- Partnered with studios to weave climate solutions into popular film narratives
- Shifted public opinion on pipeline projects through multi-pronged messaging
- Delivered narrative strategy for landmark Indigenous-led environmental challenge
- Protected Burns Bog via coordinated public pressure campaigns

Crisis Management

We weaponize research and engineer counter-narratives that don't just deflect: they destroy. Our full-spectrum approach to existential risks combines rapid crisis containment with hard-hitting research to outmaneuver adversaries and keep them on defense.

Examples:
- Helped tech clients navigate viral crises
- Coordinated progressive rapid-response teams
- Turned a far-right leadership race into a climate accountability moment
- Designed counter-disinfo ops during elections with civil society allies

Tech for Good

The right tools in the right hands at the right time can change everything. We build, source, or adapt the tech that campaigns actually need.

Examples:

Indigenous Partnership
